What Terativo Does

Terativo provides environmental, geological and geospatial intelligence to help organisations understand the risks and opportunities associated with land before making important decisions.

Environmental Intelligence

We identify environmental constraints such as protected sites, biodiversity considerations, flood exposure and land-use concerns.

Geological Insight

We screen for geological and ground-related indicators such as shrink-swell clay, alluvium, historic mining and potential instability.

Geospatial Analysis

We use GIS, satellite imagery and spatial data to assess how a site has changed and what risks may affect future development.

Questions We Help Answer

Terativo helps clients move from scattered datasets to clear, decision-ready insight.

What has happened on the land?

  • Has the site been cleared recently?
  • Has vegetation been lost?
  • Has land use changed significantly?
  • Has nearby development expanded?

Are there environmental constraints?

  • SSSI nearby?
  • Protected habitat?
  • Nature reserve?
  • Biodiversity concerns?

Are there physical risks?

  • Flood Zone 2 or 3?
  • Shrink-swell clay?
  • Historic mining?
  • Ground or slope instability?
